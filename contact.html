    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find quick answers to common questions about WandaTools.

    Is my financial data secure?

    expand_more
    Yes. WandaTools uses bank-level encryption (AES-256) to protect all your data. We comply with POPIA, South Africa's privacy law, and never share your information with third parties. Your data is yours alone.

    How much does WandaTools cost?

    expand_more
    WandaTools is free to start. You get access to Transactions, Dashboard, Documents, and WandaAI at no cost. Premium features and advanced analytics are coming soon — we'll offer flexible pricing for growing businesses.

    Can I export my data?

    expand_more
    Absolutely. You can export your transaction history as CSV, and generate professional PDF reports (Audit, Loan, Investment) at any time. Your data is never locked in.

    Is WandaAI trained on my personal data?

    expand_more
    No. WandaAI uses your data only to give you personalised insights. We don't use your financial information to train our AI model. Your privacy is protected.

    Can I integrate WandaTools with my accounting software?

    expand_more
    We're building integrations with popular SA accounting tools. For now, you can export CSV files and import into QuickBooks, Xero, or Pastel. Integration APIs are coming in Q3 2025.

    How do I delete my account?

    expand_more
    You can delete your account anytime from your Profile settings. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days, in compliance with POPIA. No questions asked.

    What payment methods do you accept?

    expand_more
    When premium features launch, we'll accept all major credit cards, EFT transfers, and mobile payment methods. We'll also offer monthly and annual billing options with discounts for annual subscriptions.

    Can I use WandaTools for my non-profit or NGO?

    expand_more
    Yes! We offer discounted pricing for registered NGOs and non-profits. Email us at support@wandatools.com with your registration details to discuss options.
