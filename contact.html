    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find quick answers to common questions about wandaPOS and wandaACC.

    Is my business data secure?

    expand_more
    Yes. Your sales and accounting data is protected in transit and at rest, and we comply with POPIA, South Africa's privacy law. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties — it's yours alone.

    Does wandaPOS work offline?

    expand_more
    Yes — offline is a core part of how wandaPOS works, not an afterthought. Sales ring up normally even without a connection (load-shedding included), and everything syncs automatically the moment you're back online.

    How does VAT get calculated?

    expand_more
    Automatically. You set your business's VAT rate once in wandaACC, and every invoice and expense splits out the VAT portion for you — no manual calculation, no spreadsheets.

    Can I export a report for a bank loan or a tax filing?

    expand_more
    Yes. wandaACC generates one-click Loan Readiness, Investment Readiness, and Tax Compliance PDF reports, alongside a full Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, and Trial Balance.

    How does wandaPOS connect to wandaACC?

    expand_more
    Every sale rung up in wandaPOS posts straight into wandaACC's ledger — no re-typing, no CSV exports to reconcile by hand.

    What happens if my device loses internet mid-sale?

    expand_more
    Nothing is lost. The sale is recorded on the device and queued for sync — as soon as connectivity returns, it's sent through automatically.

    Can wandaPOS support more than one store?

    expand_more
    Yes — wandaPOS supports multiple stores under one business, each with its own staff and till, with an owner able to see across all of them.

    I don't have any bookkeeping system yet — can I start here?

    expand_more
    Yes — that's exactly who wandaPOS and wandaACC are built for. No accounting background needed; you get a proper financial blueprint from your very first sale.

    I already keep books on spreadsheets or another system — can I switch?

    expand_more
    Yes. Plenty of businesses come to us upgrading from spreadsheets or paper books. Get in touch and we'll talk through moving your chart of accounts and opening balances over.

    How much does it cost?

    expand_more
    Pricing depends on your business size and how many stores you run. Get in touch and we'll work out what makes sense for you.
